## Overview

Nigeria's automotive market is massive but fragmented—dominated by informal networks, opaque pricing, and limited buyer protections. Mottars set out to change that by building a trusted digital marketplace where Nigerians can confidently buy, sell, and rent vehicles.

Launched in 2024 from Victoria Island, Lagos, Mottars bridges the gap between verified dealers and everyday buyers through a modern platform that prioritizes transparency, security, and convenience. The platform serves the entire automotive ecosystem: vehicle sales, auto parts sourcing, and repair services.

We partnered with Mottars from the early product stage to build a platform capable of handling the unique challenges of the Nigerian automotive market—from dealer verification to flexible payment integrations and nationwide logistics coordination.
